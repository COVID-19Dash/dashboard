Help Slow the Spread

COVID-19 is a highly infective virus that can easily spread among you and your peers.
Here are some preventive measures to protect yourself and others
from contracting the virus (provided by the Canadian Medical Association):

- Wash your hands often with soap and water (for at least 20 seconds)

- Avoid touching your face

- Stop greeting people by shaking hands, hugging or kissing

- Limit your contact with groups and close contact with people, especially those who are sick (at least 2 meters)

- Cancel all non-essential and international travel

- Clean high-touch objects and surfaces often

If you are sick:

- Stay home (even if your symptoms are mild

Cover coughs and sneezes with your elbow

Do NOT visit a clinic without calling first

How You Can Make a Difference

Here are some helpful ways you can contribute to the health of fellow Canadians, as provided by the Canadian governement:

Get the Canada COVID-19 App

The app provides updates and trusted resources for information about the virus.
You can also use the symptom tracker to log your symptons every day (even if you feel well).

Sign Up to Become a FluWatcher

FluWatcher is an online surveillance network that tracks the spread of flu-like illness across Canada.
You can participate by anonymously answering 2 quick health-related questions each week to help show Canadians where COVID-19 is circulating.

Donate Medical Supplies

This is a donation form to donate personal protective equipment or medical supplies to support frontline health care staff in Canada.
